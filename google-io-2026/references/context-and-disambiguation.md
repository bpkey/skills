# Context & Disambiguation — what was (and wasn't) at I/O 2026

Two jobs for this file:
1. **Context** — brief entries on pre-existing products that I/O 2026 announcements build on, so the landscape makes sense standalone.
2. **Disambiguation** — a guard list of things the press routinely attributes to I/O 2026 that were actually announced earlier (mostly I/O 2025) or are separate products. Google reuses brand names heavily and the 2025/2026 coverage is easy to mix up. **Check here before agreeing that something was an I/O 2026 reveal.**

---

## NOT announced at I/O 2026 (common confusions)

| Thing | Reality | Where the 2026 story actually is |
|-------|---------|----------------------------------|
| **Project Astra** | I/O **2025** "universal assistant" research banner. No new Astra announcement found for 2026. | **Gemini Spark** + **Personal Intelligence** carry the 2026 consumer-agent story. |
| **Project Mariner** | Web-browsing agent from **2025**. In 2026 it appears only as a **$200 AI Ultra** exclusive (per press). | Pricing tier perk; see `pricing-and-access.md`. |
| **Project Moohan / Samsung Galaxy XR headset** | Launched **October 21, 2025** (first Android XR headset, Snapdragon XR2+ Gen 2). Not an I/O 2026 reveal. | The 2026 XR news is the **Intelligent Eyewear (smart glasses)** — see `workspace-android-xr.md`. |
| **Veo / Imagen / Lyria / Flow** | All **I/O 2025** launches (Veo 3, Imagen 4, Lyria 2). Referenced at I/O 2026 only as *existing* tools; no new versions headlined. | The 2026 generative-media headliner is **Gemini Omni**. |
| **Jules (async coding agent)** | Launched at **I/O 2025** (Gemini 2.5 Pro). No confirmed I/O 2026 update. | 2026 dev story is **Antigravity 2.0** + **Managed Agents** + **ADK 2.0**. |
| **NotebookLM Video/Audio Overviews, Cinematic Video, Gemini Notebooks** | Real 2026 products but shipped **before** I/O 2026 (Cinematic Video ~Mar 2026; Gemini Notebooks Apr 8, 2026). | At I/O 2026, NotebookLM appears only indirectly via **"Literature Insights"** in Gemini for Science. |
| **Google Meet speech translation** | I/O **2025** feature. No verified 2026 Meet update. | — |
| **Google Photos AI editing** | No I/O 2026 Photos editing feature confirmed. | The 2026 image story is **Google Pics** + **Nano Banana**. |
| **"ADK 1.0", "Veo 3 in API", "2M-token context"** | Appear only in secondary roundups; conflict with official 2026 sources (which say **ADK 2.0**, **Gemini Omni**, **~1M context**). | Treat as unverified / earlier-year carryover. |
| **Consumer "Gemini in Chrome"** | At I/O 2026, Chrome news was mostly **SynthID/C2PA verification** + developer **WebMCP (Chrome 149)** + Chrome DevTools for Agents — not a big consumer Chrome AI feature. | See SynthID note in `foundation-models.md`. |
| **"Genie 4"** | No such product found — appears only as a speculative search echo. | **Genie 3** is the real (research-only) world model. |
| **CodeMender** *(reverse case)* | This one **was** part of the I/O 2026 Cloud story (private preview) despite thin press coverage. | See `developer-and-cloud.md`. |

## Renames you'll hear (same thing, new name)

- **Vertex AI → Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform** (rename landed at Cloud Next 2026, featured at I/O 2026). Consolidated the former **Agentspace**. Not a shutdown.
- **Gemini CLI → Antigravity CLI** (Gemini CLI retired for consumer/free use **June 18, 2026**; survives for paid Enterprise API keys).

## Sunsets (real shutdowns)

- **Firebase Studio (cloud IDE)** — sunset announced **March 19, 2026** (pre-I/O). New workspaces disabled **June 22, 2026**; full shutdown / data deletion **March 22, 2027**. Migrate to **Google AI Studio** or **Antigravity**. Firebase *backend* services are unaffected — don't confuse "Firebase Studio" (IDE, dying) with "Firebase" (backend, healthy).
