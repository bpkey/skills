# Workspace

Most "Live" features are voice-driven and gated to **AI Pro/Ultra**, U.S./English first, summer 2026.

## AI Inbox (Gmail)
- **Category:** workspace
- **What it is:** A Gmail view that surfaces what matters most — prioritization, personalized draft replies, contextual document links, dismissals.
- **What it's for:** Reducing inbox triage effort.
- **When to use it / vs alternatives:** Higher-volume inbox management vs standard Gmail.
- **Status:** **Gated** — currently AI Ultra; rolling out to AI Plus/Pro in the U.S.
- **Source:** https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/google-one/google-ai-subscriptions/
- **Announced:** May 19, 2026

## Gmail Live
- **Category:** workspace
- **What it is:** A voice conversational interface to your inbox (no thread navigation).
- **What it's for:** Querying/acting on email by voice.
- **When to use it / vs alternatives:** Hands-free email vs manual browsing.
- **Status:** **Announced-only** — summer 2026, AI Pro/Ultra (U.S., English, Android/iOS).
- **Source:** https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/google-io-2026-all-our-announcements/
- **Announced:** May 19, 2026

## Docs Live
- **Category:** workspace
- **What it is:** Create and edit documents by voice; pulls details from Gmail/Drive/Chat/web with permission.
- **What it's for:** Voice-driven document drafting.
- **When to use it / vs alternatives:** Dictation + structuring vs manual typing.
- **Status:** **Announced-only** — summer 2026, AI Pro/Ultra (global, English, Android/iOS).
- **Source:** https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/google-io-2026-all-our-announcements/
- **Announced:** May 19, 2026

## Talk to Keep
- **Category:** workspace
- **What it is:** A "brain dump" feature converting spoken thoughts into organized notes/lists in Google Keep.
- **What it's for:** Fast voice capture into structured notes.
- **When to use it / vs alternatives:** Quick capture vs manual note-taking.
- **Status:** **Announced-only** — summer 2026, AI Pro/Ultra + preview for Workspace business (Android).
- **Source:** https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/google-io-2026-all-our-announcements/
- **Announced:** May 19, 2026

## Google Pics
- **Category:** workspace (image creation)
- **What it is:** An AI image creation/editing tool built on the latest **Nano Banana** model — object segmentation, text editing/translation, blank-canvas or edit-existing, Workspace integration.
- **What it's for:** Generating and editing images inside Google's productivity surfaces.
- **When to use it / vs alternatives:** Still-image generation/editing within Workspace. For **video**, use **Gemini Omni** (see `foundation-models.md`). Note: this is distinct from **Google Photos** — no I/O 2026 Photos editing feature was confirmed.
- **Status:** **Gated → Announced** — limited trusted testers now; summer 2026 global rollout to AI Pro/Ultra + preview for Workspace business.
- **Source:** https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/google-io-2026-all-our-announcements/
- **Announced:** May 19, 2026

# Android

> Caveat: much Android 17 detail came from the earlier **Android Show (May 12, 2026)** and press, not the official I/O 2026 roundup. Treat per-feature specifics as press-sourced unless confirmed.

## Gemini in Android 17
- **Category:** android
- **What it is:** "Gemini Intelligence" integrated into Android 17, demoed doing cross-app tasks (e.g. find a syllabus in Gmail, identify the textbooks, add them to a cart). Android 17 also adds **App Bubbles** (floating windows).
- **What it's for:** OS-level agentic assistance across apps.
- **When to use it / vs alternatives:** System-wide automation vs single-app actions.
- **Status:** **Announced** (Android 17); per-feature availability not detailed in the official I/O roundup.
- **Source:** https://9to5google.com/2026/05/19/google-io-2026-news/ ; https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/12/everything-google-announced-at-its-android-show-from-googlebooks-to-vibe-coded-widgets/ *(press-sourced)*
- **Announced:** ~May 12–19, 2026

## Android Halo
- **Category:** android
- **What it is:** A new top-of-screen UI space showing agent task progress/updates without interrupting current activity.
- **What it's for:** Surfacing background agent (e.g. Gemini Spark) status at a glance.
- **When to use it / vs alternatives:** Monitoring running agents.
- **Status:** **Announced-only** — coming later in 2026, for Gemini Spark and supported agents.
- **Source:** https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/sundar-pichai-io-2026/ ; https://9to5google.com/2026/05/19/google-io-2026-news/
- **Announced:** May 19, 2026

# Android XR

## Android XR — Intelligent Eyewear
- **Category:** android-xr
- **What it is:** Two glasses types — **audio glasses** (spoken help) and **display glasses** (on-lens info) — with Gemini, built with Samsung/Qualcomm and designed with **Gentle Monster** and **Warby Parker**; compatible with Android and iPhone.
- **What it's for:** Hands-free, on-the-go Gemini assistance.
- **When to use it / vs alternatives:** Ambient/visual assistance vs pulling out a phone.
- **Status:** **Announced-only** — arriving **fall 2026**.
- **Source:** https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/google-io-2026-all-our-announcements/ ; https://9to5google.com/2026/05/19/google-io-2026-news/
- **Announced:** May 19, 2026
- **Note:** The **Project Moohan / Samsung Galaxy XR headset** is a *separate, earlier* product — it launched **October 21, 2025**, not at I/O 2026. The 2026 XR news is the glasses. See `context-and-disambiguation.md`.
